# Contract Domain Entity Types

## PARTY
Organization or individual that is a signatory or referenced entity in a contract.

## CONTRACT
A formal agreement between parties, identified by contract number or title.

## OBLIGATION
A required action, delivery, or compliance requirement specified in a contract.

## DEADLINE
A date or time constraint for an obligation or deliverable.

## COST
A monetary amount, fee, or payment term.

## VENUE
A physical location referenced in a contract.

## SERVICE
A service being provided under contract.

## INSURANCE
Insurance requirement or coverage specification.

## PENALTY
A consequence for breach or non-compliance.
