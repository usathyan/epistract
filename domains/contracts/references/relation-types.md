# Contract Domain Relation Types

## OBLIGATED_TO
Party is obligated to fulfill an obligation.

## HAS_DEADLINE
Obligation or deliverable has a deadline.

## COSTS
Service or obligation has an associated cost.

## SIGNED_BY
Contract is signed by a party.

## PROVIDES_SERVICE
Party provides a service under contract.

## HELD_AT
Event or service takes place at a venue.

## REQUIRES_INSURANCE
Contract requires insurance coverage.

## CROSS_REFERENCES
One contract references or depends on another.

## PENALIZES
Breach of obligation triggers a penalty.
